Linear Partial Differential Equations

Because the formulation is much simpler than others, the extension to other problems is straightforward.

  • Laplace/Poisson problems (electrostatics)
  • Stokes flows
  • Dynamics of Point Vortices
  • Linear Elastic Problem
  • Gravitational Many Body Problem (Astrophysics)
  • Bubbles in Potential flows
